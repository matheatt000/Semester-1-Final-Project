Tips and Tricks Guide

Rock Paper Scissors

In Rock Paper Scissors, remember that rock beats scissors, scissors beat paper, and paper beats rock. Try to predict your opponent's next move based on their previous choices!

Guessing Game

In the Guessing Game, start by guessing a number in the middle of the range. Use each guess to narrow down the possibilities based on whether your guess was too high or too low.

Aim Training Game

In the Aim Training Game, focus on your hand-eye coordination. Try to stay relaxed and keep your movements smooth for better accuracy.
